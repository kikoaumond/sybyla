Live at Austin City Limits Festival by Northern Irish singer-songwriter Van Morrison is a limited edition live album recorded from the Austin City Limits Festival concert at which he was the first night headliner on September 15, 2006. It has only been made available at live Van Morrison concerts and at the Van Morrison Official website.

The September appearance at the Austin City Limits Festival was at the closing end of a tour to promote his 2006 released country and western album Pay the Devil. The tour had begun on March 7, 2006 with Morrison's first ever concert at the famed Ryman Auditorium.[1]

His performance at the festival was rated as one of the 10 Best Shows at Austin City Limits by Rolling Stone Magazine and the two disc CD features the complete concert.[2]
